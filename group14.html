WeCan (Student managed NGO in NIT Agartala)
Various blood donation camps are organized by WeCan every year. Blood donation camps are usually organized by blood banks, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and government agencies. These camps provide a convenient way for people to donate blood and help save lives. They also raise awareness about the importance of blood donation and encourage more people to become regular blood donors. WeCan aims at providing quality education to poor students and those who are in need of guidance and mentorship at higher level to cross various competitive examinations such as JEE Mains and JEE Advanced. Apart from that this student body also raises awareness among poor segment of the society and make them feel the importance of education. WeCan also raises awareness about pre existing myths and taboos of the society. This student body holds weekly tours and sessions to motivate and aware some of the most backward regions of North-Eastern India, especially in NIT Agartala where communal diversity is at its peak.
WeCan contributes to the North Eastern infantry education from primary level till senior secondary. Here at WeCan, we aim to provide quality education to poor students through proper quality assets. We are self running college based NGO run by student body council. All those students who are interested to teach and contribute something to the society join hands together to run this beautiful legacy.
